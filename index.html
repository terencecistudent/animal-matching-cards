Animal Matching Card Game

How to play!

Click the Start Game button to start the timer and game!

You will have 40 seconds to complete the game!

Click on two cards at a time to see if you get a match.

Once all the cards are matched, press the New Game button to play again!

Additional Device Size Information:

If playing on iPad or phone, it is best to play it on landscape!

Seconds Left:
Congratulations!
To play again, just press the New Game button!

Unlucky! You are out of time!

To play again, just press the New Game button!
